# YNOR UNIFIED AXIOMS

## Purpose
This document is the unique canonical source for the Ynor axiom layer.
It consolidates the former foundational and secondary strata into a two-level hierarchy:
- Level 1: Foundations
- Level 2: Specialized Domains

## Level 1: Foundations

### A1. Center
Ynor has a unique chiastic center from which every branch can be read.
The center is the point of convergence, return, and global coherence.

### A2. Mirror
Every Ynor branch can have a mirror, return, or active symmetry.
Oppositions are treated as organized reflections rather than breaks.

### A3. Fractality
Any Ynor structure may reproduce itself at smaller or larger scales without losing identity.
This enables recursive expansion without dissolution.

### A4. Recursivity
Any Ynor output may become an input again.
Reading is therefore non-linear and cyclic by design.

### A5. Coherence
Any Ynor expansion is valid only if it remains readable, connected, and non-contradictory with the core.
Growth without relation is treated as structural loss.

### A6. Differentiation
Ynor branches must remain distinguishable while still belonging to the same whole.
Unity does not imply uniformity.

### A7. Transformation
Ynor is versioned, reformulated, recomposed, and refined over time.
Revision is part of validity, not a deviation from it.

### A8. Verifiability
Every Ynor claim must be traceable to a source, procedure, test, or recorded observation.
Unfounded assertions do not belong to the hard core.

### A9. Transmission
Ynor must be transmissible without losing structure or orientation.
Readability and documentary memory are conditions of durability.

### A10. Interdependence
The elements of Ynor derive meaning from their relations.
No axis is absolutely isolated.

### A11. Plurality
Ynor accepts multiple reading modes, levels, and descriptive regimes.
Excessive reduction is avoided when it destroys meaning.

### A12. Responsibility
Reading, writing, and diffusion of Ynor must be handled with precision and care.
Form quality is part of usage quality.
