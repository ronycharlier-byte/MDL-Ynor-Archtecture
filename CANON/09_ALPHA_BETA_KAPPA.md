# Alpha, beta, kappa

Question:
- What is the relation between alpha, beta and kappa?

Answer:
- `alpha` is canonical force and useful density.
- `beta` is documentary noise and duplicate pressure.
- `kappa` is internal friction and maintenance debt.
- `mu = alpha - beta - kappa`.

- `alpha` = canonical force, useful density, doctrinal authority
- `beta` = documentary noise, duplicates, archives, mirrors, pseudo-sources
- `kappa` = internal friction, encoding damage, naming instability, maintenance debt

Relation:
- `mu = alpha - beta - kappa`
- increasing `alpha` without lowering `beta` and `kappa` is fake progress
