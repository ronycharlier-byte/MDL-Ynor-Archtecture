# Alpha, beta, kappa

- `alpha` is stabilization capacity and useful density.
- `beta` is documentary noise, duplicate pressure, and mirror pressure.
- `kappa` is maintenance debt, encoding damage, and naming instability.

Relation:
- `mu = alpha - beta - kappa`

Operational rule:
- increasing `alpha` without reducing `beta` and `kappa` is fake progress.

Measurement hint:
- `alpha` is inferred from recovery and citation retention.
- `beta` is inferred from duplication, leakage, and drift.
- `kappa` is inferred from repair cost, parsing damage, and naming friction.
