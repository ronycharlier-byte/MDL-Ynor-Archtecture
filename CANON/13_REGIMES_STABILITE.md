# Stability regimes

Regimes:
- `mu <= 0` -> collapse
- `0 < mu < 3` -> fragile
- `3 <= mu < 5` -> serious RAG
- `mu >= 5` -> production window

Interpretation:
- the regime is determined by survival margin, not by visual polish;
- the thresholds are calibration bands and should be re-estimated when the corpus changes;
- they are provisional and convention-based, not demonstrated universal laws;
- `dot mu` matters as much as `mu` because slope change often precedes collapse.
