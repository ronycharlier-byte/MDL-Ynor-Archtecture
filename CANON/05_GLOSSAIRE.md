# Glossaire

- `alpha`: effective stabilization capacity.
- `beta`: documentary noise, duplicate pressure, mirror pressure.
- `kappa`: maintenance debt, encoding damage, naming instability.
- `mu`: `alpha - beta - kappa`.
- `dot mu`: temporal derivative of `mu`, estimated on a window.
- `epsilon`: perturbation amplitude or bounded external shock.
- `S(t)`: corpus or system state at time `t`.
- `E`: amplification operator.
- `D`: projection to the safe domain.
- `M`: memory or inertia operator.
- `canonical_id`: stable authority identifier for one canonical source.
- `citation_id`: stable citation handle for one resolvable section or chunk.
- `concept_id`: stable semantic concept label.
- `document_role`: precise documentary function.
- `source_of_truth`: document or layer that owns the value.
- `retrieval_weight`: relative retrieval priority, not a truth score.
- `layer`: governance layer.
- `status`: lifecycle or admissibility status.
