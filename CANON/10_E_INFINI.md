# E infinity law

Query anchors:
- Explique la loi e infini proportionnelle a epsilon sur mu.

Question:
- How does the e infinity law work?

Answer:
- `e_infinity proportional to epsilon / mu`
- as `mu` falls, small errors amplify
- when `mu` is low or negative, retrieval noise becomes structural risk
