# E infinity law

Risk law:
- `e_infinity proportional to epsilon / mu`

Reading:
- as `mu` falls, small errors amplify;
- when `mu` is low or negative, retrieval noise becomes a structural risk.

Status:
- this is a warning law, not a performance target.
- `epsilon` must be bounded and observed; it is not an excuse for infinite assumptions.
