# Journal targeting

## Primary target

### Foundations of Physics

Best fit for the manuscript because the paper focuses on quantum foundations,
ontology, and the emergence of classical structure.

Recommended submission file:

- `MANUSCRIT_FINAL_FOUNDATIONS_OF_PHYSICS.md`
- `METADONNEES_SOUMISSION.md`

## Backup targets

### Synthese

Strong fit if the paper is framed more explicitly as a philosophy of science and
structural realism contribution.

### British Journal for the Philosophy of Science

Good fit if the comparison with rival interpretations is emphasized.

### Philosophy of Science

Viable if the paper is sharpened as a conceptual and methodological intervention.

## Short editorial pitch

This manuscript proposes a minimal structural ontology for quantum reality. It
combines an ontological framework, a formal sketch grounded in standard quantum
formalism, and a methodological principle (`mu`) that selects admissible
ontological components by balancing explanatory power, ontological cost, and
interpretative friction.

## Editorial guardrails

- Present the paper as a structural ontology and methodological contribution.
- Keep formal coherence, local validation, and external review distinct.
- Use the targeted version when the audience is physics-first.
- Avoid presenting the manuscript as a completed experimental result.
